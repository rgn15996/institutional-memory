# Objections Log — Gloucester Air

*From discovery calls on 2026-05-28 and 2026-06-09. SYNTHETIC DEMO DATA.*

## O1 — "We just spent 18 months on ServiceNow. We are not replacing anything." (Fiona Aldridge, CIO)

**Severity: high.** Fiona hears "platform" and assumes rip-and-replace.

**Our counter:** OASIS is integration-agnostic and sits *above* the existing
estate as a unifying layer. ServiceNow stays the system of record; OASIS
orchestrates across it. Frame it as "making the ServiceNow investment work
harder", never as an alternative. This landed well on the second call.

oversight and the ROSTA crew-scheduling system: "no robot restarts that."

**Our counter (partial):** Human-in-the-loop by design — agents propose,
humans approve for designated critical systems. We do NOT yet have the
per-action audit evidence pack he asked for. Flagged to DXC product team;
awaiting response. Do not re-raise with Douglas until we have it.
