A DXC-flavoured take on Card D (Sales Engineer). All data is **synthetic** —
Gloucester Air, every named person, Meridian AIOps, Calder IT Services, and
all figures are fictional.

(DXC's agentic IT operations platform — a unifying layer above the
customer's existing tools: automation at scale with human judgement in the
loop, real-time estate-wide visibility, predictive insight, traceable ROI)
to Gloucester Air, a fictional UK regional carrier.
